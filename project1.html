Project Overview

PROS is a B2B cloud software company that helps competitive enterprises create a frictionless and personalized buying experiences for customers.
During my internship, the company is transforming its legacy products into modern commerce. So I collaborated with senior UX designers, UX researchers, developers and data scientists on several projects, specially designed a new product for generating sales agreements, Price Agreement for SellingPRO.
My main contributions are ideation and iteratively design, I came up with 2 solutions to different scenarios and was able to create 5+ prototypes, which promotes the company’s product transformation.

What is Price Agreement for SellingPRO?

Price Agreement for SellingPRO is a new product concept aiming to help enterprises proceed in generating price agreements for their customers and close more deals.
This new product is going to be part of the company's SellingPRO , which is a modern commerce platform that enables companies to deliver a personalized sales experience using eCommerce tools, including Microsoft CRM.
As more customers start to use eCommerce to be time efficient in the sales process, it is important for the Price Agreement to provide convenient as well as personalized service.

What is Price Agreement for SellingPRO?

As the company is transforming legacy products to modern commerce ,in this phase, there are three main goals for this project:

  • Fasten the sales turn-around time between sales representatives and customers.
  • Connect other products on SellingPRO platform to provide a frictionless sales process for customers.
  • Seeking opportunities to integrate A.I. to personalize sales experience.
